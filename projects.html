Projects

Highlights of projects Nichola has led are documented below to showcase what NM CONSULTING INC. can do for your organization:

New Physician Onboarding Checklist

June - September 2019

NM CONSULTING INC. worked in collaboration with the Ridge Meadows Hospital Physician Engagement Society (RMHPES) and the Ridge Meadows Hospital (RMH) Administration to develop a New Physician Onboarding Checklist and welcome package. This onboarding checklist and welcome package is available to newly hired physicians at RMH and complements the recently developed RMHPES Orientation Package. The Onboarding Checklist is especially useful to new physicians who are unfamiliar with the community and who do not have any pre-existing ties or physician mentors currently located at the hospital, but also may be used for new medical staff joining the RMH health care team.

Core services:

  • Recruitment & retention
  • Strategic communications planning
  • Program development

First Metropolitan United Church Strategic Planning Session

May - June 2019

Twenty-two members of the First Metropolitan United Church (FMUC) congregation, including the Lead Minister, members of the FMUC Board and individuals church lay leadership positions, met to participate in a Strategic Planning Session facilitated by NM CONSULTING INC. This was the first strategic planning session undertaken by the new FMUC Board. During the session, NM CONSULTING INC. assisted participants in determining the FMUC strategic priorities for the next three to five years, facilitated a networking opportunity for participants, and enabled the FMUC leadership team to have a positive experience in developing an actionable Strategic Plan with attainable results and measurable outcomes.

Core services:

  • Strategic planning
  • Presentation development & delivery
  • Report writing
  • Meeting facilitation

Development of Committee Terms of Reference and Evaluation Tracker Tool

April - May 2019

NM CONSULTING INC. assisted the Forensic Psychiatrist Working Group develop a formal Committee Terms of Reference as well as an evaluation tracker tool to assist with quarterly project assessments. The Terms of Reference established the purpose, scope, limitations, membership, meeting schedule and expected outcomes for the Forensic Psychiatrist Working Group, while the evaluation tracker tool was a set of evaluation questions to be consistently asked regarding each activity that the Working Group undertakes.

Core services:

  • Service review & analysis
  • Committee development
  • Performance measurement
  • Reporting & evaluation

Summary of Canadian Cross-Jurisdictional Forensic Psychiatrist to Patient Ratios Report

February - April 2019

The Forensic Physician Engagement Society (FPES) engaged NM CONSULTING INC. to produce a report examining comparative information on Forensic Psychiatrist to patient ratios occurring in similar cross-jurisdictional forensic facilities and BC in-patient tertiary psychiatry programs in order to create a guideline on an appropriate Forensic Psychiatrist to patient ratio.

Core services:

  • Service reviews & analysis
  • Report writing & editing
  • Interviews & meeting facilitation
  • Qualitative analysis & research

Orientation Guide Development

January 2019

Working with the Board of the Ridge Meadows Hospital Physician Engagement Society (RMHPES), NM CONSULTING INC. developed an Orientation Guide that provided a general introduction and background information on the RMHPES. The Orientation Guide is a useful tool to educate new Board Members, physicians considering working at Ridge Meadows Hospital (RMH), new RMHPES members, new RHM Administration, contracted staff or any other individual wanting to learn more about RMHPES.

Core services:

  • Recruitment & retention
  • Strategic communications planning
  • Program development

Pacific Centre Family Services Association

February 2019

A joint Board and senior management strategic planning session was facilitated by NM CONSULTING INC. whereby content was created to build a three-year strategic plan for the non-profit organizations focussed on providing counselling and other supports to individuals and families living in the Westshore. After in-depth review of the achievements and challenges, NM CONSULTING INC.led participants through a S.W.O.T.eR Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats & Expected Results), which resulted in an insightful discussion and revision of the Mission, Vision and Goals statements. NM CONSULTING INC. then guided participants through a prioritizing exercise regarding Pacific Centre’s prime focus categories. The session concluded with a brainstorming discussion on “Enhancing What We Currently Do.”

Core services:

  • Strategic planning
  • Presentation development & delivery
  • Report writing
  • Meeting facilitation

Optimal Forensic Psychiatrist Structure for Review Board Clinical Evidence Gathering and Organizational Interaction Project

September – December 2018

Working with the Forensic Physician Engagement Society, this project had two main objectives – to examine structures to assist Forensic Psychiatrists in the gathering and presentation of Review Board evidence; as well as determining a structure for Forensic Psychiatrists to engage and interact with the BC Mental Health & Substance Usage Services organization. The project was organized into three phases: gathering internal perspective; cross jurisdictional review; and recommendation development based on learnings. The intent of the project was to provide information to be used for educational and discussion purposes on a go-forward basis.

Core services:

  • Service review & analysis
  • Interviews
  • Report writing
  • Presentation development & delivery
  • Qualitative Research

Ridge Meadows Hospital Physician Engagement Society Visioning Session

October – December 2018

The Board of the Ridge Meadows Hospital Physician Engagement Society utilized the facilitation services of NM CONSULTING INC. to lead them through a visioning and strategic planning retreat. The session enabled the Board and their strategic partners to discuss past successes and challenges and how learnings may inform future priorities. The Society’s vision, mission and goals were reviewed and improved to reflect the current state, and future funding priorities and activities were planned. Information gathered at the Visioning Session was translated into a 2019-2021 Strategic Plan.

Core services:

  • Strategic planning
  • Presentation development & delivery
  • Meeting facilitation
  • Report writing & editing

Provincial Health Services Authority Medical Staff Structure Organization Review

November 2017 - May 2018

This project included a strategic planning process to examine the medical staff organization and identified changes to the current structure, including the review of leadership positions, committees, physician roles and responsibilities, and working relationships.

Core services:

  • Leadership assessment
  • Strategic planning
  • Service reviews & analysis
  • Presentation development & delivery
  • Report writing & editing
  • Interviews, focus groups & meeting facilitation
  • Quantitative & qualitative analysis and research
