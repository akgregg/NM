Projects

Highlights of projects Nichola has led are documented below to showcase what NM CONSULTING INC. can do for your organization:

Ridge Meadows Hospital Physician Engagement Society Visioning Session

October – December 2018

The Board of the Ridge Meadows Hospital Physician Engagement Society utilized the facilitation services of NM CONSULTING INC. to lead them through a visioning and strategic planning retreat. The session enabled the Board and their strategic partners to discuss past successes and challenges and how learnings may inform future priorities. The Society’s vision, mission and goals were reviewed and improved to reflect the current state, and future funding priorities and activities were planned. Information gathered at the Visioning Session was translated into a 2019-2021 Strategic Plan.

Core services:

  • Strategic planning
  • Presentation development & delivery
  • Meeting facilitation
  • Report writing & editing

Provincial Health Services Authority Medical Staff Structure Organization Review

November 2017 - May 2018

This project included a strategic planning process to examine the medical staff organization and identified changes to the current structure, including the review of leadership positions, committees, physician roles and responsibilities, and working relationships.

Core services:

  • Leadership assessment
  • Strategic planning
  • Service reviews & analysis
  • Presentation development & delivery
  • Report writing & editing
  • Interviews, focus groups & meeting facilitation
  • Quantitative & qualitative analysis and research

Single Parent Employment Initiative

April 2015 - November 2017

The Single Parent Employment Initiative (SPEI) is a unique training and return-to-work employment program that helps break the cycle of welfare and facilitates improved livelihoods for both the single parent and their children.

Core services:

  • Business case & policy development
  • Program development, implementation & monitoring
  • Issues management
  • Strategic communications planning

Services to Adults with Developmental Disabilities Initiative

March 2014 - November 2017

The Services to Adults with Developmental Disabilities (STADD) Initiative is an integrated service delivery model that assists the individual and their family in planning for transition from youth to adulthood.

Core services:

  • Strategic planning
  • Change management
  • Program development, implementation & monitoring
  • Issues management
  • Business case & policy development
  • Performance measurement, reporting & evaluation

Technology@Work – Program Implementation

May 2015 - November 2016

The Technology@Work program was established to support people with work-related barriers due to disability or functional limitation who require assistive technology for employment or volunteer activities.

Core services:

  • Business case & policy development
  • Program development, implementation & monitoring
