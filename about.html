About

Collaboration | Teamwork | Relationships | Partnerships

NM Consulting Inc. is an incorporated company established in 2017 with headquarters located in Victoria, British Columbia. Nichola Manning is the President and owner/operator of NM Consulting Inc.

Nichola believes in public sector excellence and is passionate about assisting organizations to improve performance and achieve outcomes that will make a difference to the livelihood of their clients. Her leadership philosophy is focused on empowerment, inclusion and collaborative relationships. Nichola has comprehensive understanding of government models including language and political environments. She is knowledgeable in a variety of facilitation methods, is an excellent listener, will focus on outcomes, and is flexible in meeting the needs of the client.
