Nichola Manning

BA, MPA
President
NM CONSULTING INC.

Prior to establishing NM CONSULTING INC., Nichola worked in the BC provincial government for 20 years, where she led large-scale organizations and associated programs.



Nichola spent 16 years with the Ministry of Health (MOH). As an Assistant Deputy Minister of the Medical Services Health Human Resources Division, Nichola had oversight for primary health care reform, the Medical Services Plan, and physician negotiations. In addition, she was responsible for establishing and maintaining the strategic relationships among government, physicians, health authorities, and physician organizations.



As a senior health executive, Nichola garnered experience chairing many high-profile physician, health authority and government committees, such as: the General Practice Services Committee, Specialist Services Committee, Shared Care Committee, MOH/Health Authority VPs Medicine Committee, amongst others. As well, Nichola oversaw the development and implementation of many health programs and initiatives, such as: Physician Master Agreement, Divisions of Family Practice, the Patient Attachment Initiative, the Nurse Practitioners for BC program, the Patients as Partners Initiative, and the Lifetime Prevention Schedule.



In September 2013, Nichola was appointed Assistant Deputy Minister of the Employment and Labour Market Services Division at the Ministry of Social Development and Social Innovation. Here she provided leadership to the nationally recognized WorkBC employment program, assisting unemployed British Columbians return to the workforce. Nichola developed a comprehensive understanding of employment programming, overseeing $300 million in WorkBC Employment Service Center contracts with not-for-profit and private employment agencies. She was also a member of the BC Labour Market Priorities Board that had responsibility to implement the BC Jobs Plan and Skills for Jobs Blueprint.
